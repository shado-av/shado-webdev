Welcome to SHADO!

Welcome to the Simulator of Humans and Automation in Dispatch Operations (SHADO)! This tool allows you to rapidly prototype dispatch operations of transportation systems. SHADO simulates the work of dispatchers and fleet managers under various operating conditions during a shift. With SHADO, you can tune numerous parameters, run the simulation for multiple days, then see the distribution of operator workload and error as well as system delays.

You should use this tool to answer the following questions:
  • When are my operators over or under-utilized at work?
  • Why are my operators over or under-utilized at work?
  • How might we improve operator workload, as well as overall system efficiency and safety?
